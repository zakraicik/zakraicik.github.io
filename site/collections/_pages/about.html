Approach to Data Science

My name is Zak and I'm not afraid to say I'm a data science nerd. However, just because I don't mind getting my hands dirty and getting lost in some code doesn't mean that's where my passions lie. In fact, I am most passionate about the intersection of data science and business strategy. I believe that data science is a tool that can be used to help companies operate more efficiently, make better decisions, and uncover unique insights. This belief is the product of a variety of different positions across many functional areas. 

Skills

I'm a self trained data scientist who started my career in finance. I've spent time in excel spreadsheets, word documents, and powerpoints as well as identifying, developing and testing hypotheses. I've built models ranging in complexity. I've deployed models to the cloud, although, I will admit I am more comfortable leaving this step to the machine learning engineers. Below is a list of skills I have mastered

  • Supervised Learning: Linear/Logistic Regression, SVM, Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, XGBoost, KNN, Naive Bayes
  • Unsupervised Learning: Hierarchical and K-Means clustering, PCA
  • Deep Learning: Neural Networks, Tensor Flow, Keras
  • Programming: Java, Python, SQL, MATLAB, and familiar with: C/C++, MPI
  • Cloud Computing: AWS, Azure

Just because I've turned 30, doesn't mean I need to stop learning, right? I'm purusing a Master's degree in Computer Science and Information Technology from the University of Pennsylvania - partly because I love to learn and partly because I recognized holes in my self taught data science skill set. This degree gives me the chance to formalize my knowledge of both computers and machine learning concepts. Fight on, Pennsylvania!

Fun Fact

I'm based in San Diego, CA. I'm a coffee-drinking, golf-playing, early-to-bed kind of guy. I thrive outside of my comfort zone. I enjoy a nice routine, but am not afraid to break it. My love for numbers goes beyond the desk - check out my Instagram for a complete rating of burgers, salads and lobster rolls.
